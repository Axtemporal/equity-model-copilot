# Corporate Life Cycle — LLM Wiki

The **concept-oriented** knowledge base distilled from Aswath Damodaran's
*The Corporate Life Cycle*. One article per concept, cross-linked, with
provenance to the source slides/papers/posts. This is the navigable companion to
the *by-chapter* notes (`../chapters/cap_01…cap_20`) and the synthesis
([[00_framework_lifecycle]], [[application_to_copilot]]).

**How to read it.** Start at the hub — [[corporate-lifecycle]] — and follow its
cross-links. Every article leads with a one-sentence definition, explains the
concept, shows how it shifts across the life cycle, and ends with provenance.
The article contract is in [`_schema.md`](_schema.md); the canonical concept list
in [`_concepts.md`](_concepts.md).

> Compliance: frameworks are described, never turned into investment advice; all
> content is distilled (no verbatim copying); sources are public.
